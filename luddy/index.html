Luddy's Adventure

A Ludum Dare Game

Help Luddy find the secret cave. Collect other bugs along the way to increase your score.

HINT: Green Bugs are magical and give you special powers to see caves. Neat right!

HINT: Doors lead places

HINT: Yellow Blocks in caves are sand. They lead places sometimes too.

HINT: There's a Boss Fight... Nice right... Oh and you're invicible and I ran out of time to spend any time on the art for the boss.

How to Play

  • Move: Arrow Keys - up, down, left, right
  • Fire: Press Spacebar

Explore the world and find the Green Bug to find the Secret caves
